Chicken Cake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 Whole Chicken
  • 8 Cups Flour
  • 9 Tablespoons Baking Soda
  • 8 Cups Water

Instructions

  1. 1. Mix Chicken and Flour in a Large Bowl
  2. 2. Slowly Stir In Baking Soda
  3. 3. Add Water
  4. 4. Thouroughly blend (takes 3-5 hours)
  5. 5. Cook at 525 for 3 Hours
